How to create an invoice

  1. Fill in the form. Add your business, your client, line items, and any tax or discount. A live preview updates as you type.
  2. Style it. Pick a template, add your logo and an accent color, and optionally a QR code — or keep the clean default.
  3. Copy the link. InvoiceIguana compresses the whole invoice into a URL. That link is the invoice — there is nothing else to save.
  4. Share it. Send the link by email, text, or chat. Your client opens it and their browser rebuilds the invoice — then they can print or save it as a PDF.

Frequently asked questions

Is the invoice generator really free?

Yes. It's free with no signup, no account, and no watermark on your invoice. Because the whole invoice lives in the link, there's no per-document server cost.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There's no signup and no login. Open the page, fill in the form, and share the link that's generated — that link is the invoice.

Where is my invoice data stored?

Nowhere on our servers. The invoice is encoded into the part of the URL after the #, which browsers never send to a server. Your client's browser rebuilds it locally.

Can I add my logo, tax, and discounts?

Yes — a logo, accent color, QR code, flat or percentage tax, an overall discount, and per-line discounts. A live preview shows exactly what your client sees.

How do I turn the invoice into a PDF?

Open the invoice link and use your browser's print dialog, then choose Save as PDF. The invoice is print-ready.

Can I edit an invoice later?

Yes. Every result includes an edit link that reopens the form with all your data and style choices, so you can tweak it or reuse it as a template.

What about receipts?
